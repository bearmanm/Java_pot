Arrowline Travel are specialists in small group travel.

Arrowline Travel

Based in Salisbury, the company was established in 2000 with the aim to meet the need of groups who were too large to travel in a single taxi but not large enough to require a coach which are invariably financially unviable for small parties. By offering chauffeur driven People Carriers for between 1-7 passengers and 8, 12 and 16 seater minibuses we are able to offer a vehicle which best suits your requirements and pocket. Each vehicle is priced at a different tariff according to size.

All our drivers are uniformed, courteous and professional, hold the relevant PCV qualifications and are DBS checked. We pride ourselves on offering a reliable and personal service from your first enquiry through to the end of your journey. Our friendly and attentive staff will be happy to quote for any occasion. Whatever your journey, you can be rest assured you will receive quality, value for money, and the highest level of customer service from everyone at Salisbury Minibuses.
